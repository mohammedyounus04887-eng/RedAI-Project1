1. Executive Summary
RED AI Academy is India's most intensive AI skilling ecosystem — 12+ specialized tracks, 120 hours of live instruction, and learning centers across Hyderabad, Bengaluru, Raipur, Delhi, Pune, and Ahmedabad. As the academy scales nationally, the absence of a unified intelligent management system creates operational friction across inquiry handling, batch scheduling, fee collection, and learner progress tracking.

This document presents a comprehensive plan to build the RED AI Academy Management System (RAMS) — a fully integrated, AI-powered platform that manages every dimension of academy operations. Eight core modules cover the full learner lifecycle from first inquiry to placement, with Claude AI embedded throughout.
